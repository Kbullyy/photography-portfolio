About Me

Hello! I'm Catilin, born and raised in the crossroads of the Hoosier state, Indiana. I am 25 years young aspiring to become a full time photographer! I have had experience shooting events such as sports, weddings,

senior photos, as well as prom pictures. I am willing to explore more categories in photography and expand my horizons of experience.

I am a passionate photographer dedicated to capturing life's most beautiful moments. Whether it's a high energy sports, an intimate portrait, or a special event, my goal is to capture images that create amazing

memores. With 5 years of experience and a deep love for the art of photography, I strive to provide my clients with unforgettable memories.
